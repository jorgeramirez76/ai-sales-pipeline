The Predictable Revenue framework

Aaron Ross's Predictable Revenue (co-authored with Marylou Tyler, 2011) codified modern outbound B2B. Core principles: (1) specialize roles — SDRs book meetings, AEs close deals, never both; (2) Cold Calling 2.0 — use email + LinkedIn for introductions, not cold phone dials; (3) Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — define narrowly and target ruthlessly; (4) target the 'right' person — not the CEO, but the actual decision-influencer; (5) outbound = compounding pipeline — predictable, measurable, scalable.

How your Custom AI Brain applies it

The AI runs the SDR function: ICP-targeted outbound, multi-touch email + LinkedIn sequences, meeting booking, qualification. When meetings book, they hand to your human AEs. The SDR/AE separation is preserved — AI doesn't try to close deals; it books qualified meetings for humans to close. This is the highest-ROI AI use case for SaaS and B2B services.

Cold Calling 2.0 at scale

Cold Calling 2.0 uses email + LinkedIn, not phone dials. The first touch is a short email asking for a referral ('who's the right person at your company to talk to about X?') rather than a pitch. AI runs this sequence across hundreds of accounts simultaneously — volume humans can't match while maintaining personalization.

ICP precision matters most

Ross's deepest insight: the best outbound results come from narrow ICP targeting. AI helps enforce discipline — it won't outreach to out-of-ICP accounts just because the list is longer. The AI is configured with your exact ICP (company size, vertical, role, tech stack, funding stage) and filters aggressively.

Frequently asked questions

Is this endorsed by Aaron Ross or Predictable Revenue Inc?

No. We're not affiliated with Aaron Ross, Marylou Tyler, or Predictable Revenue Inc. We apply the publicly-taught methodology from the 2011 book and subsequent content.

Does Predictable Revenue work for small teams?

The methodology scales down. You don't need a 20-person SDR team — AI effectively gives you 1 virtual SDR's output at a fraction of the cost. For a solo founder or 2-person team, AI-as-SDR is how you generate outbound pipeline without hiring yet.

How does AI handle LinkedIn outreach?

Via LinkedIn Sales Navigator + approved API integrations. AI sends personalized messages at human-reasonable pace (not mass-spam volume) to stay within LinkedIn's usage policies. For higher volume, we recommend combining LinkedIn with email to avoid LinkedIn rate limits.

What about inbound leads — does the AI handle those differently?

Yes. Inbound (someone filled out your form, visited your pricing page) has different dynamics than cold outbound. Inbound AI focuses on fast response + BANT qualification. Outbound AI focuses on ICP targeting + introduction-seeking. Both workflows supported, usually in the same account.
