Frequently asked questions

Are you endorsed by these sales thinkers?

No — we're not affiliated with Hormozi, Miner, Robbins, Ferry, or any other named methodology creator. We build AI that applies their publicly-taught frameworks, combined with your specific business. Think 'AI that understands the Hormozi playbook' not 'AI Hormozi'.

Can I mix methodologies?

Yes — Custom AI Brain supports hybrid approaches. A common powerful stack: NEPQ questioning to uncover real needs + Robbins' Six Human Needs to identify psychological drivers + Hormozi's Grand Slam Offer framework to structure the proposal. The AI sequences these naturally, not as three separate bots.

How long does Custom AI Brain training take?

3-5 days for publicly-documented methodologies (Hormozi, NEPQ, Robbins, Ferry). 5-7 days for custom proprietary methodologies where we train on your own recorded conversations + playbook. We need ~50 ideal-conversation examples to train effectively.

What if my sales approach is my own, not a named framework?

Even better — that's what Custom AI Brain is designed for. Give us 50 recordings of your best conversations, your scripts, your objection handling playbook, and your typical closer. We train the AI to sound like you applying your method — not a famous person applying theirs.

Does persona training work for non-sales use cases?

Yes — customer service, recruiting, internal HR, executive assistants. The persona is just 'how this AI thinks and speaks.' We've trained AIs on internal playbooks for support teams, sales engineering teams, and executive assistants with great results.
