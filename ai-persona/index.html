Are you endorsed by these sales thinkers?

No. We're not affiliated with Hormozi, Miner/7th Level, Robbins/RRI, Tom Ferry International, Cardone, Mulrenin, Keller Williams corporate, VaynerMedia, Holmes estate, Kennedy/GKIC, ClickFunnels/Brunson, or Mark Cuban. We build AI that applies their publicly-taught frameworks — 'AI that runs the Hormozi playbook' not 'AI Hormozi.'

Can I mix methodologies?

Yes. A common powerful stack: NEPQ questioning + Robbins' Six Human Needs + Hormozi's Grand Slam Offer + Cardone-level follow-up volume. The AI sequences these naturally, not as four separate bots.

What if my sales approach is proprietary?

Give us 50+ recordings of your best conversations, your scripts, and your objection-handling playbook. We train the AI on YOUR method — not a famous person's. Many of our best Custom AI Brains are 100% proprietary.

Does persona training work for non-sales use cases?

Yes. Customer service, recruiting, internal HR, executive assistants. The persona is 'how this AI thinks and speaks.' Training works identically for any human-facing communication role.
