NEPQ Scripts for Real Estate Agents: Convert More Leads

Published March 28, 2026 · AI Sales Pipeline · 8 min read

AI CRM Real Estate Automation Lead Generation

NEPQ (Neuro-Emotional Persuasion Questions) is a sales methodology developed by Jeremy Miner that focuses on asking questions to uncover emotional motivation rather than pushing features. In real estate, NEPQ scripts dramatically outperform traditional cold calling scripts — and AI Sales Pipeline has them built in.

What is NEPQ?

NEPQ stands for Neuro-Emotional Persuasion Questioning. Developed by Jeremy Miner, it's a sales methodology built on the insight that people buy based on emotion and justify with logic. NEPQ questions are designed to:

  • Lower the prospect's defensive posture (no pushy sales feeling)
  • Uncover the emotional "why" behind their real estate decision
  • Make the prospect sell themselves on taking action
  • Create urgency through their own answers, not your pitch

In real estate, NEPQ is the difference between a cold call that ends in 30 seconds and a conversation that ends with a listing appointment.

NEPQ vs Traditional Real Estate Scripts

Traditional script: "Hi, I'm [Name] with [Brokerage]. I see your home has been on the market for 60 days and the listing expired. I'm a specialist in this area and I'd love to get together and show you how I can get your home sold. Are you available Tuesday or Wednesday?"

NEPQ approach: "Hi [Name] — I noticed your listing came off the market recently. I'm just curious — was that a deliberate decision or did things just not work out the way you hoped?"

The NEPQ approach doesn't ask for anything. It asks a question. The prospect has to engage. And the answer they give reveals their motivation, frustration, and openness — all the information you need to have a productive conversation.

Key NEPQ Principles for Real Estate

1. Status Questions (Softeners)

Start with "I'm just curious..." or "Can I ask you something?" These phrases lower resistance before asking your real question.

2. Situation Questions

Understand where they are: "How long have you been thinking about selling?" / "What prompted you to start looking now?"

3. Problem Awareness Questions

Surface the pain: "What's been the most frustrating part of the process so far?" / "What happened with the previous listing that you wish had gone differently?"

4. Consequence Questions

Make the cost of inaction real: "If you don't get this sold in the next 60 days, what happens with your plans?" / "What's riding on finding the right home by [timeframe]?"

5. Qualifying Questions

Confirm they're the decision maker: "Besides yourself, is there anyone else involved in this decision?" / "If we found the right property this week, is there anything preventing you from moving forward?"

What is NEPQ in real estate?

NEPQ (Neuro-Emotional Persuasion Questions) is a sales methodology by Jeremy Miner that uses specific question types to uncover emotional motivation, lower resistance, and guide prospects to self-identify why they should take action. It's highly effective for real estate prospecting and lead conversion.
