Pricing Questions

What's included in the $4,995 setup fee?▼
Everything. Your complete system configuration, all lead source integrations (Zillow, Realtor.com, Facebook, Google, Espresso Agent, IDX Broker, etc.), pre-loaded NEPQ and Reverse Selling workflow sequences, custom AI voice training, landing page builds, and a live onboarding call where we walk you and your team through the entire system.
Is there a contract? Can I cancel?▼
No contract. Month-to-month billing on all plans. You can cancel anytime, but we're confident you won't want to once you see the results. If you choose annual billing, you'll lock in significant savings (10-20% discount).
Are there any hidden fees or overage charges?▼
No. Your monthly plan includes all SMS, email, voice, and AI features. Unlimited contacts, unlimited users, unlimited campaigns. No per-contact fees. No overage charges. The price you see is the price you pay.
How many leads/month is each plan designed for?▼
Basic: 50-150 leads/month. Pro: 100-500+ leads/month. Elite: 500+ leads/month across unlimited sub-accounts. These are guidelines, not hard limits.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?▼
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. If you upgrade, we'll prorate the difference. If you downgrade, we'll credit the overage to your next billing cycle. No penalties.
What payment methods do you accept?▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and ACH transfers for annual plans. Invoicing available for annual contracts. Payment is processed securely through Stripe.
Is there a free trial?▼
We offer a personalized demo and consultation (free) to see if AI Sales Pipeline is right for you. Once you decide to move forward, we do a 14-day custom setup. The setup fee ($4,995) is a one-time investment.
What happens if the system doesn't work for my market?▼
We have a 90-day money-back guarantee on the setup fee. If you don't see measurable results within 90 days (following our recommendations), we refund the $4,995. No questions asked.
