AI Sales Pipeline vs Lofty (Chime) — Real Estate AI CRM Comparison

Lofty (formerly Chime) is one of the most ambitious real-estate CRMs in 2026 — AI features, IDX websites, lead gen included. We overlap on AI. Here's where each wins.

Home › Compare › Lofty (formerly Chime)

What Lofty does well

Lofty combines CRM + IDX website + lead gen (they'll sell you Facebook and Google leads) + AI features into one platform. For agents who want one vendor for everything, Lofty's 'all in one' approach is compelling. Their IDX websites are legitimately good. Pricing: ~$500-$1,500/mo depending on lead volume and features.

What Lofty's AI actually does

Lofty's AI, as of 2026, focuses on: AI-suggested email responses, basic SMS auto-reply for initial contact, lead scoring based on engagement, and some AI content generation for marketing. It's solid but limited — the AI is 'suggest-then-human-sends' rather than 'autonomous-AI-handles-the-conversation-end-to-end'.

What AI Sales Pipeline does differently

Our AI is autonomous end-to-end. We train a Custom AI Brain on your voice, methodology, and specific playbook. The AI holds multi-turn conversations without human approval of each message. It handles voice calls (outbound and inbound). It adapts to methodologies like NEPQ, Hormozi Grand Slam Offers, or Mulrenin Reverse Selling. Lofty's AI assists the agent; our AI replaces the first 80% of outreach work.

Lead gen vs AI focus

Lofty includes lead gen (they'll sell you leads). AI Sales Pipeline does NOT sell leads — we're the outreach layer that processes leads you generate. If you're already happy with your lead sources (Zillow, Facebook ads, referrals), you don't need Lofty's lead gen. If you need lead gen too, Lofty's bundled model is simpler. We integrate with 20+ lead sources to ingest whatever you're running.

Which to pick

Lofty: if you want one vendor for website + CRM + lead gen + light AI, and you're a medium-sized team that values simplicity over depth. AI Sales Pipeline: if you already have lead sources and need the outreach execution layer — or if you want AI that sounds like you (not a generic bot). Many agents use both (Lofty for IDX + CRM, AI Sales Pipeline for the actual conversation work).

Frequently asked questions

Can I use my Lofty IDX website with AI Sales Pipeline?

Yes. Your Lofty IDX site generates leads; those leads flow into AI Sales Pipeline for the outreach layer. This is a common setup — you keep the Lofty front-end, we provide the execution back-end.

Is Lofty's AI getting better?

Yes — they've invested heavily in AI features in 2024-2026, and some of our overlap is closing. But their AI architecture is 'assist the agent' vs our 'replace the agent's first 80% of work.' These are meaningfully different products.

Which one is cheaper?

Similar starting price ($497-$500). Lofty scales up faster because lead-volume pricing tiers. AI Sales Pipeline has flatter pricing (most users stay on Pro at $695/mo or Elite at $995/mo regardless of lead volume).

What about kvCORE (sister product to Lofty)?

Similar comparison applies. kvCORE focuses on websites + lead gen + CRM; weaker on autonomous AI. If you're on kvCORE, AI Sales Pipeline complements it the same way as Lofty.
