Frequently asked questions

Which real estate CRM is best?

Depends on your team size, what you already have, and where your pain is. For solo agents wanting AI execution: AI Sales Pipeline. For large teams wanting one-vendor simplicity: Lofty or BoomTown. For organizational/coordination focus: Follow Up Boss. For long-term agents comfortable with a mature product: Top Producer.

Can I use AI Sales Pipeline alongside my current CRM?

Yes — we integrate with Follow Up Boss, Lofty, BoomTown, Top Producer, and most major real estate CRMs. Many teams run both: CRM as the database, AI Sales Pipeline as the AI execution layer on top.
