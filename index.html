What is AI Sales Pipeline?

AI Sales Pipeline is an AI-powered CRM and lead follow-up system built specifically for real estate agents. It automates your entire sales process — from the moment a lead enters your pipeline through conversion — using AI text messaging, AI email sequences, and AI voice calling. Unlike generic CRMs like HubSpot or general automation tools like GoHighLevel, every feature is purpose-built for how real estate agents actually work.

The platform responds to new leads in under 60 seconds, qualifies them using AI lead scoring, and follows up persistently across SMS, email, and voice channels using NEPQ psychology-based scripts. It even generates personalized AI clone videos of you — your face, your voice, your mannerisms — customized for each individual lead.

With specialized modules for FSBO automation and expired listing automation, plus seller workflows and buyer workflows, AI Sales Pipeline helps agents save 15-20+ hours per week on follow-up while dramatically increasing conversion rates. See pricing or compare to other CRMs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Clone Video actually work? ▼
We use cutting-edge AI video generation to create a lifelike video of you. You record a sample video, our AI learns your facial expressions and voice patterns, and then generates personalized videos for each lead. They'll hear their name, see you addressing them directly, and feel like they got a personal message — because they did.
Can I customize the messaging and workflows? ▼
Absolutely. Every workflow is fully customizable. You can adjust messaging, timing, triggers, and sequences. We also have pre-built templates for FSBO, Expired, Downsizer, and Buyer workflows that you can modify to fit your style.
What integrations do you support? ▼
We integrate with Zillow, Follow Up Boss, Pipedrive, HubSpot, Google Sheets, Facebook Ads, and more. If you need an integration with another platform, let us know — we're constantly adding new connections.
How long does it take to set up? ▼
Most agents are up and running in 24 hours. We handle the integration, you provide some basic setup info, and our team gets your workflows configured. For more complex setups, we offer white-glove onboarding.
Is this compliant with FTC regulations? ▼
Yes. Our system includes opt-out mechanisms, clear disclosures about automated outreach, and compliance tools to ensure you meet all FTC, TCPA, and GDPR requirements. We're constantly updated as regulations evolve.
Can I use this for both buyer and seller leads? ▼
Yes. We have specialized workflows for both. Our system can intelligently route leads based on their intent, and you can customize your approach for buyers vs. sellers within the same dashboard. See our seller workflows and buyer workflows feature pages for details.
How is AI Sales Pipeline different from GoHighLevel or HubSpot? ▼
GoHighLevel is a generic business automation tool. HubSpot is an enterprise CRM. Neither is built for real estate. AI Sales Pipeline is purpose-built for real estate agents with pre-loaded NEPQ scripts, real estate lead source integrations (Zillow, Realtor.com, IDX), FSBO and expired listing automation modules, and AI that responds to leads in 60 seconds. See the full comparison.
How much does AI Sales Pipeline cost? ▼
Plans start at $497/month for solo agents (Basic), $695/month for growing agents and teams (Pro), and $995/month for brokerages and teams (Elite). There is a one-time $4,995 setup fee that covers full system configuration, AI training, integrations, and personalized onboarding. We offer a 90-day money-back guarantee on the setup fee. See full pricing details.
Does AI Sales Pipeline work for FSBO and expired listing leads? ▼
Yes — these are two of our strongest features. The FSBO automation module integrates with Espresso Agent for daily FSBO data import, then runs power dialer calls, voicemail drops, and NEPQ SMS sequences automatically. The expired listing module does the same for expired listings, starting outreach the morning a listing expires. Learn about FSBO automation or expired listing automation.
