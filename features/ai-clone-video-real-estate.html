Your AI Twin, Working 24/7

Video is the most engaging form of communication. But recording personalized videos for every lead is impossible. Until now.

AI Clone Video creates a lifelike digital version of you. You record a short training video, and our AI learns your facial expressions, voice patterns, speaking style, and mannerisms. Then it generates unlimited personalized videos — each one customized with the lead's name, their specific interests, and your message.

The result? Leads receive a video that looks and sounds exactly like you recorded it just for them. They'll see you saying their name, referencing their property search, and addressing their specific situation. It's personal at a scale that was never possible before.

How It Works

🎬 One-Time Training

Record a 2-minute sample video. Our AI analyzes your face, voice, expressions, and speaking patterns to create your digital twin.

✨ Dynamic Generation

For each lead, the AI generates a unique video with their name, their property interests, and personalized messaging — all in your voice and likeness.

📧 Multi-Channel Delivery

Videos are embedded in emails, sent via SMS, or shared through any channel. Each video gets a unique link for tracking.

📊 Engagement Tracking

See who watched, how long they watched, and when they engaged. Use this data to prioritize your hottest leads.

🔄 Trigger-Based

Videos send automatically based on lead behavior — new inquiry, property view, price drop on a saved listing, etc.

🔒 Privacy & Ethics

Full disclosure that the video is AI-generated. Transparent, ethical, and compliant with all regulations.
