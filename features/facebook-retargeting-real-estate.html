Stop Advertising to Strangers

Traditional Facebook ads spray and pray — showing your message to thousands of people who aren't interested. It's expensive and inefficient.

Retargeting is different. These people already visited your website. They viewed your listings. They checked your pricing. They're interested — they just didn't take action yet. These are the highest-value prospects you can advertise to.

Our AI automatically builds retargeting audiences based on website behavior, then creates and manages Facebook campaigns to bring those visitors back. It optimizes creative, budget, and targeting automatically. You don't touch a thing.

How It Works

📊 Audience Building

The system automatically creates Facebook Custom Audiences from your website visitors, segmented by behavior — listing viewers, pricing page visitors, contact form abandoners.

🎨 Dynamic Creative

AI generates ad creative featuring the exact listings each person viewed. They see what they cared about, not generic ads.

💰 Smart Budget

The system allocates budget to the highest-performing audiences and ads automatically. No wasted spend.

📈 Performance Tracking

See exactly which ads drive appointments and closings. The AI optimizes for actual revenue, not just clicks.

🔄 Sequential Messaging

Different messages for different stages — first-time visitors see different ads than people who viewed 5+ listings.

⏰ Frequency Capping

The AI prevents ad fatigue by controlling how often people see your ads. Effective without being annoying.

Audience Segments

All Website Visitors: Broad retargeting for general awareness and brand recall.

Listing Viewers: People who viewed specific properties — highest intent, show them similar listings.

Pricing Page Visitors: Serious prospects evaluating cost — conversion-focused messaging.

Contact Form Abandoners: Almost converted — gentle nudge to complete the form or call.

Engaged Users: High activity visitors — VIP treatment with exclusive content or early access.
