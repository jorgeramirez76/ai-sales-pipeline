Why AI SMS Changes Everything

Speed wins in real estate. When a lead fills out a form on Zillow, Facebook, or your website, they're actively thinking about buying or selling right now. Wait 30 minutes to respond, and they've already moved on. Wait 5 minutes, and three other agents have already contacted them.

AI SMS eliminates that gap entirely. The moment a lead enters your pipeline, they receive a personalized text message — tailored to their specific inquiry, referencing their property interest, and sounding completely human. Not a generic "Thanks for your interest" autoresponder. A real conversation starter.

Our AI analyzes the lead's source, the property they're interested in, and their behavior to craft the perfect opening message. Then it maintains the conversation, answering questions, qualifying the lead, and booking appointments — all via text.

Key Capabilities

⚡ Instant Response

Leads receive a text within 60 seconds of entering your pipeline. While competitors are still checking their email, you've already started the conversation.

🎯 Personalized Messages

Every text references the specific property, neighborhood, or listing that caught their interest. The AI reads their inquiry and responds accordingly.

🔄 Smart Follow-Up

If they don't reply, the AI sends perfectly-timed follow-ups. Not too pushy, not too distant. Just the right cadence to stay top-of-mind.

Leads can reply and the AI responds intelligently. It answers common questions, handles objections, and escalates hot leads to you immediately.

🕐 24/7 Availability

Leads don't only submit inquiries during business hours. Your AI SMS works while you sleep, on weekends, during holidays — never missing an opportunity.
