What Is Real Estate Seller Lead Automation?

Seller lead automation uses AI to run customized sequences for different seller types. FSBO sellers receive Reverse Selling sequences (value-first, not pitch-first). Expired listing owners receive sequences addressing why their listing failed and how your approach is different. Downsizers, commuters, divorce/estate/relocation leads each receive sequences tailored to their life situation and psychology. Every sequence uses NEPQ language (identifying Need, Emotion, Problem, Question) to build rapport and position you as the solution. Result: FSBOs become clients, expired listings get re-listed, life events become conversions.

The 6 Seller Workflows

Each workflow is customized to a specific seller type and motivation.

💡

FSBO (For Sale By Owner)

Reverse Selling approach: Provide value first. Market analysis, pricing comparisons, common FSBO mistakes. By day 3, they see why agents matter. 40% of FSBOs become clients when they realize selling alone is harder than they thought.

📉

Expired Listings

Problem-first messaging: "Your listing expired. Why?" Address: wrong pricing, poor marketing, bad timing. Share new market data. Present your proven approach. Expired owners are motivated. They want to sell. They just need a better strategy.

📦

Downsizers

Life event targeting: Empty nesters, relocations, lifestyle changes. Sequences address their motivations: simplifying, freeing capital, changing community. Empathetic messaging + your services = high conversion.

✈️

Commuters

Relocation sequences: New job, new city, tired of commuting. Sequences provide community info, market data, timeline acceleration. Commuters are urgent. They need to move fast.

💔

Divorce/Estate/Relocation

Sensitive, empathetic approach: Divorce, inheritance, family relocation. These are emotional, not transactional. Sequences handle sensitivity while accelerating timelines. Highest conversion rates because motivation is strong.

🎯

Custom Workflows

Your methodology: Train workflows on your scripts, your market, your philosophy. Brandon Mulrenin Reverse Selling, Jeremy Miner NEPQ, Tom Ferry, or your own approach. Fully customizable.

FSBO leads receive Reverse Selling approach: AI doesn't immediately pitch your services. Instead, AI provides value first (market reports, pricing comparisons, common FSBO mistakes). By day 3, FSBO sees why agents matter. Conversion: 40% of FSBOs become clients.
What is the Expired Listings workflow? ▼
Expired listing owners receive sequences addressing: why their listing failed (wrong pricing, poor marketing, timing), new market data, and your proven approach to get it sold. NEPQ sequences highlight their pain, then position you as the solution.

Downsizers (empty nesters, relocations, divorce) receive sequences addressing life events and motivations. AI recognizes these signals (large home for small family, relocating) and speaks to their unique situation with empathy and solutions.
Can workflows be customized? ▼
Yes. Each workflow uses your scripts, your market data, your philosophy. Train AI on Brandon Mulrenin Reverse Selling, Jeremy Miner NEPQ, Tom Ferry, or your own approach. Workflows speak your language.
