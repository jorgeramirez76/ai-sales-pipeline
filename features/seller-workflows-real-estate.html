Sellers Need Different Messaging

FSBOs think they can do it themselves. Expired listings are frustrated and skeptical. Downsizers have emotional attachments and logistical concerns. Each group requires a completely different approach.

Our seller workflows are built by studying thousands of successful conversions. We know exactly what messaging works for each seller type, when to send it, and how to overcome their specific objections.

The AI doesn't just send generic seller content. It understands the psychology of each segment and adapts accordingly. The result? Higher response rates, more appointments, and more listings.

Workflow Types

🏠 FSBO Automation

For sale by owner leads need education, not persuasion. The workflow delivers market data, pricing insights, and subtle proof that agents deliver better results — without being pushy.

📉 Expired Listing

Expired sellers are frustrated. This workflow acknowledges their experience, identifies what went wrong, and positions you as the solution — with empathy first.

👴 Downsizer Campaign

Empty nesters and retirees have emotional attachments and practical concerns. The workflow addresses both with sensitivity and practical guidance.

📊 Market Report

Regular neighborhood market updates keep you top-of-mind with potential sellers. Automated, personalized, and highly effective for future listings.

💰 Equity Alert

Automatically notify homeowners when their equity hits certain thresholds or when market conditions favor selling. Strike while motivation is high.

🎯 Referral Request

Systematic follow-up with past clients and SOI for referrals. Stay connected without being annoying.

What Makes These Work
