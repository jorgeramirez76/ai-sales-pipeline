The Power of Voice at Scale

Nothing builds rapport faster than a phone call. But you can't call every lead personally — you'd never sleep. AI Voice solves this impossible tradeoff.

Our AI voice technology doesn't sound robotic. It has natural pauses, understands context, handles interruptions, and responds intelligently to questions. Leads often can't tell they're talking to AI until you tell them.

The AI follows your script, asks qualifying questions, handles common objections, and seamlessly transfers hot leads to you or books appointments directly on your calendar. It never gets tired, never has a bad day, and never forgets to make a call.

Key Capabilities

🗣️ Natural Conversations

Advanced speech synthesis with emotional range, natural pacing, and the ability to handle interruptions. Sounds like a real person, not a robot.

🎧 Intelligent Listening

Real-time speech recognition that understands context, sentiment, and intent. The AI adapts its responses based on what the lead actually says.

📅 Appointment Booking

When a lead is interested, the AI checks your calendar in real-time and books appointments directly. No phone tag, no back-and-forth.

🔥 Hot Lead Transfer

When a lead shows high intent or asks complex questions, the AI can transfer to you immediately with full context of the conversation.

📝 Call Summaries

Every call is transcribed and summarized. You'll know exactly what was discussed, objections raised, and next steps — without listening to recordings.

⚡ Instant Follow-Up

After the call, the AI automatically sends a text or email summary to the lead, confirming what was discussed and next steps.

Sample Call Flow

AI: "Hi, this is Jorge with AI Sales Pipeline. I saw you were looking at homes in Springfield online. Are you still looking to buy in that area?"

Lead: "Yeah, actually we are. We've been looking for a few weeks now."

AI: "That's great! What's been the biggest challenge finding the right place so far?"

Lead: "Everything either needs too much work or it's way overpriced."

AI: "I hear that a lot. There are actually some great move-in ready homes that just hit the market this week. Would you be open to seeing a couple this Saturday morning?"

The AI continues the conversation, qualifies their timeline, pre-approval status, and books the showing.
