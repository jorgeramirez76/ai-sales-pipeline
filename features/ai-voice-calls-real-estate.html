How AI Voice Calls Work

Intelligent outbound calling that sounds like a real agent—because it's powered by your sales playbook.

1

AI Dials Lead

Lead list is fed to AI. AI places calls intelligently—optimal times, respect do-not-call, manage cadence. When lead answers, AI greets them naturally with your intro and immediately establishes purpose.

2

AI Listens & Qualifies

AI actively listens to the lead, asks qualifying questions (motivation, timeline, budget, competition), and understands intent. Real-time sentiment analysis tells AI if lead is interested, skeptical, or dismissive.

3

AI Handles Objections

Lead says "I'm not interested"? AI responds conversationally, pulls contextual rebuttals from your sales scripts, and pivots. Objection handling is trained on your market, your approach, your voice.

4

AI Books or Escalates

Hot lead? AI books appointment directly into calendar and sends confirmation. Warm lead? AI gets contact info for later follow-up. Cold lead? AI closes professionally and logs outcome for AI refinement.
