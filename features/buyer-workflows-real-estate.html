Buyers Need Speed and Relevance

Buyers are emotional and time-sensitive. They see a listing they love, they want information NOW. Wait an hour, and they've moved on to the next agent. Send generic listings, and they tune you out.

Buyer workflows are designed for the unique psychology of home shoppers. Fast response, relevant listings, and intelligent qualification that happens automatically. The AI learns what each buyer wants and delivers exactly that.

Whether they're first-time buyers nervous about the process, investors looking for deals, or move-up buyers with specific needs — the workflow adapts to their situation and timeline.

Workflow Capabilities

⚡ Instant Response

Buyer inquiries trigger immediate, personalized responses. While other agents are still checking their voicemail, you've already sent relevant listings.

🔍 Smart Matching

The AI analyzes their search criteria and behavior to recommend listings they'll actually love — not just what's in their price range.

💰 Qualification

Gentle but effective pre-approval and budget qualification built into the conversation. Know who's serious before you show houses.

📅 Showing Booking

Qualified buyers can book showings directly through the AI. It checks your calendar, confirms the appointment, and sends reminders.

📊 Market Education

First-time buyers get educational content about the process. Investors get ROI data and market trends. Relevant to their needs.

🔄 Long-Term Nurture

Not ready to buy yet? The workflow keeps them engaged with market updates, new listings, and helpful content until they're ready.

Buyer Journey Stages

Discovery: Just browsing — educational content, market insights, no pressure

Active Search: Looking seriously — frequent listing alerts, showing availability, neighborhood guides

Pre-Approved: Ready to buy — priority access to new listings, off-market opportunities, fast scheduling

Under Contract: Deal in progress — inspection reminders, milestone check-ins, vendor recommendations

Post-Close: New homeowner — move-in resources, referral requests, future investment opportunities
