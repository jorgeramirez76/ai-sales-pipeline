Beyond Basic Drip Campaigns

Traditional email automation sends the same sequence to everyone. It's better than nothing, but it's not intelligent. AI Email is different.

Our system tracks every interaction — opens, clicks, replies, website visits, property views — and adapts the sequence accordingly. A lead who opens every email and clicks on listings gets a different path than someone who hasn't engaged in weeks. The AI knows the difference and responds appropriately.

Each email is dynamically personalized with the lead's name, their property interests, their timeline, and even references to previous conversations. It doesn't feel like marketing. It feels like you remembered them.

How AI Email Works

🧠 Behavioral Triggers

The AI monitors what leads do, not just what they say. Viewed three listings in one neighborhood? The next email focuses on that area. Haven't opened emails in two weeks? It adjusts the subject line strategy.

✍️ Dynamic Content

Every email is generated fresh based on current data. Market updates relevant to their search area. New listings that match their criteria. Price drops on properties they've viewed.

⏰ Perfect Timing

The AI learns when each lead is most likely to open emails. Morning person? They get emails at 8 AM. Night owl? 8 PM. Each lead gets optimized delivery times.

🔄 Smart Escalation

When a lead shows high engagement — clicking multiple links, replying, viewing the same property repeatedly — the AI escalates them for immediate personal follow-up.

📊 A/B Testing

The system constantly tests subject lines, content, and timing to optimize performance. Every email gets smarter over time.

🎯 Multi-Channel Sync

Email works alongside SMS and voice. If a lead responds to a text, the email sequence pauses appropriately. Everything stays coordinated.

Sample Sequence: New Buyer Lead

Day 0 (immediate): Welcome email with 3 listings matching their criteria, plus a local market report for their target area.

Day 2: "Questions buyers in [Neighborhood] are asking" — educational content that positions you as the local expert.

Day 5: Market update: "3 new listings just hit [Area] this week" — creates urgency and keeps them engaged.

Day 8: Social proof: "How the Martinez family found their home in [Neighborhood]" — success story relevant to their search.

Day 12: Personal check-in: "Still looking in [Area]? I have some off-market opportunities..." — direct but valuable.

The sequence continues for months, nurturing the relationship until they're ready to move.
