Frequently asked questions

Can AI diagnose?

No. AI never diagnoses. It triages urgency (emergency vs routine vs informational), books appointments, handles administrative. Medical judgment is always the vet. This is explicit in the AI's configuration.

What about after-hours emergencies?

AI is trained on emergency triage decision trees. If a caller describes potential poisoning, trauma, respiratory distress, labor complications, etc., AI immediately routes to the on-call vet or nearest emergency clinic. Non-emergencies get next-day appointments.

Does AI integrate with our practice management system?

Yes — eVetPractice, AVImark, Cornerstone, Impromed, ezyVet are directly supported. Appointments sync to your schedule, patient records update, prescription requests route through normal approval.

Can AI handle multi-location clinic networks?

Yes. Elite tier supports per-location routing (new patients go to the nearest location), shared specialty referrals (dental surgery at location B), and unified reporting across locations.
