Frequently asked questions

Does AI understand pool-specific terminology?

Yes — we train the AI on pool industry language: fiberglass vs concrete vs vinyl, skimmer baskets, cartridge filters, variable-speed pumps, salt systems vs chlorine, heaters (gas, heat pump, solar). The AI sounds like a pool professional, not a generic bot.

Can AI handle service dispatch for unusual issues?

AI handles 80% of service requests (standard issues). Unusual situations (suspected leak, tile/coping damage, electrical issues) get triaged to a human estimator because they require on-site diagnosis.

What about new-build consultations?

AI handles inquiry-to-consultation. On-site design consultation stays with the builder. AI's job is getting qualified prospects (budget, timeline, property-fit) to the consultation; the builder designs and closes.

Does AI work for commercial pool services (HOAs, hotels)?

Yes — with different workflows. HOA/hotel service is relationship-sales; AI handles maintenance scheduling, compliance documentation, status updates. Not primary sales. For commercial new-build (rare), relationship + RFP sales still requires human ownership.
