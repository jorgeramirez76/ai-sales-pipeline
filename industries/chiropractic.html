Frequently asked questions

Is AI HIPAA-compliant for chiropractic?

Yes — Elite tier includes BAA. Medical intake handling is encrypted and audit-logged. New-patient booking without medical data doesn't trigger HIPAA at the same level; once symptoms and history are collected, Elite-tier handling applies.

Can AI handle cash vs insurance pricing?

Yes. AI asks about insurance first; for uninsured or cash patients, shares cash-pay rates (set by your practice). For insurance, AI verifies network participation and quotes estimated out-of-pocket.

Does AI work for specialty chiropractic (sports, prenatal, pediatric)?

Yes, with specialty-specific intake. Sports chiro needs injury mechanism info. Prenatal asks about due date and specific concerns. Pediatric handles child-specific questions from parent. Configured per specialty.

How does AI handle treatment plan sales?

The AI describes the recommended plan based on the doctor's exam findings, shares pricing transparently, answers financing questions, and schedules the next appointment. Closing (patient commits to plan) often happens after the exam when the doctor personally explains findings — AI handles the logistics around it.
