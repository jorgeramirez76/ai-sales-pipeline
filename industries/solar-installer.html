Why solar installers struggle with lead follow-up

Solar leads are expensive and fragile

Cost-per-lead runs $80-$300 for qualified residential solar inquiries. A slow response burns that investment. AI locks in the lead within 60 seconds.

Qualification math confuses buyers

'Will this pay for itself?' depends on utility rate, roof orientation, tax credit, financing. AI walks buyers through the math conversationally, not a scary spreadsheet.

Site surveys eat your setter's day

Each in-home survey is 2-3 hours. Booking 5 unqualified surveys a week = 15 hours of wasted setter time. AI qualifies first so only serious prospects get booked.

Federal tax credit questions derail conversations

'Can I use the 30% federal credit?' 'What if I don't have enough tax liability?' These questions kill momentum when the setter isn't there. AI handles them 24/7.

Frequently asked questions

Can AI actually calculate solar savings accurately?

AI gives directionally-correct estimates (plus or minus 15%) based on the buyer's current bill, local utility rate, and solar irradiance for their zip code. For a binding proposal, the in-home survey is still needed — the AI's job is to get the buyer confident enough in the math to book that survey.

What about roofs that aren't good for solar?

AI runs a rough fit-check based on roof age (older than 15 years may need replacement first), material (shake/tile is harder than asphalt), orientation (south-facing is best), and shade (if buyer mentions significant tree shade, flagged as complex). Non-fit leads get routed to a 'check back in 18 months' nurture sequence instead of a survey booking.

How does AI handle financing objections?

Financing is the #1 objection in solar. AI addresses it by walking through the three main paths: cash purchase (30% federal credit + ownership), loan (still own system, monthly payment usually less than current electric bill), lease (no upfront cost, but no credit and no ownership). Most buyers self-select into the path that fits their situation when shown the math transparently.

Does AI Sales Pipeline integrate with Aurora or OpenSolar?

Yes — we push qualified leads to design tools (Aurora, OpenSolar, Solargraf) with the basic intake data pre-populated, so your designer opens a partially-completed project. Final roof modeling and proposal generation still happens in the specialized design tool.

What about commercial solar?

Commercial solar is a different workflow — 6-18 month sales cycles, CFO-level stakeholders, capital approvals, PPA vs ownership structure. AI Sales Pipeline supports commercial via custom configuration on the Elite tier, but commercial is primarily relationship + engineering sales, not AI-led. Residential is where our system dramatically outperforms human teams.

Can AI handle door-knock follow-up?

Yes. Door-knockers collect basic info on-site (name, phone, bill size, interest level), which flows into the AI system the same way. AI then runs follow-up for the 60% of doors that said 'send me more info' and never got a real human callback. Typical result: 10-20% of previously-dead door leads convert to site survey.
