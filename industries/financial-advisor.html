Frequently asked questions

Is AI Sales Pipeline FINRA/SEC compliant?

The platform supports FINRA/SEC-compliant workflows: archived communications, pre-approved message templates, automatic insertion of required disclosures (past performance disclaimer, registered-with-SEC, etc.). Your compliance officer reviews and pre-approves the templates. The AI cannot send messages that haven't passed the compliance review. Communications are archived per Rule 17a-4.

Can AI make investment recommendations?

No — AI Sales Pipeline does not make specific investment recommendations, and is configured to NOT do so. The AI's scope is lead qualification, scheduling, and client service communication (appointment reminders, annual review outreach). Any investment advice conversations route to the licensed advisor.

Does AI work for fee-only vs commission advisors?

Both. The compliance rules differ slightly (fee-only = fiduciary standard, commission = suitability + Reg BI), which affects disclosure language. AI templates are configured per firm based on your registration and affiliations.

What about high-net-worth prospecting?

HNW prospecting ($5M+) is different — it's relationship-intensive, referral-only, and AI's role is limited to administrative (scheduling, reminders, pre-meeting prep briefs). For core advisory ($250K-$5M), AI can meaningfully drive new client acquisition.

Can AI handle annual client reviews?

Yes — the highest-ROI workflow for established advisors. AI runs the annual review outreach 30/60/90 days before the anniversary, sends the pre-review questionnaire, schedules the meeting, and follows up post-meeting with action items. Typical result: advisors using this go from ~60% of clients reviewed annually to ~90%+.
