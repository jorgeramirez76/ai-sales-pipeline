Frequently asked questions

Is AI Sales Pipeline HIPAA-compliant?

The platform supports HIPAA-appropriate workflows for health and Medicare agencies — encrypted data handling, audit logs, BAA (Business Associate Agreement) available on the Elite tier. Standard insurance (P&C, life) doesn't require HIPAA compliance at the same level; the regulatory framework there is state DOI + carrier-appointed rules, which we handle via workflow guardrails.

Can AI quote policies or just collect info for a quote?

Depends on the carrier. For captive agents (State Farm, Allstate, Nationwide), AI typically collects info and routes to the carrier's rater — final quote is produced by the carrier system. For independent agents with multi-carrier rating platforms (QuoteRush, PL Rating, Rocket Referrals), AI can deliver a preliminary cross-carrier quote in minutes and route to the agent for final binding.

Does AI handle Medicare AEP surge?

Yes — AEP (Oct 15 - Dec 7) is the use case where AI dramatically outperforms human teams. During AEP, lead volume goes up 3-5x, and agents are often booked out 2-3 weeks for in-person sales meetings. AI handles the initial qualification, plan comparison, and appointment scheduling at unlimited concurrent scale — so agents spend face-time only with AEP-ready buyers.

What about cross-selling my existing book?

The highest-ROI application for existing agencies. AI runs annual policy review outreach (policy anniversary dates), identifies cross-sell opportunities (home-only clients who need auto, auto-only who need life), and books the review call. Typical result: 8-15% of contacted book books a review call; 30-50% of review calls produce a new policy sale.

Can AI handle claims inquiries?

Yes for triage and non-urgent questions. AI distinguishes claims types (auto fender-bender vs house fire vs death claim), routes true emergencies to the carrier's 24/7 line, and handles the straightforward 'what's my claim number' or 'when will my payment be issued' questions without agent involvement. Complex claims always route to a human.

Does AI replace my Customer Service Rep (CSR)?

No — it augments them. The CSR handles the 20% of inquiries that need human judgment (complex coverage questions, cancellation saves, claim advocacy); AI handles the 80% that are routine. Agencies using AI typically redeploy CSR time to relationship-building activities, not cut headcount.
