Why mortgage brokers struggle with lead follow-up

Rocket Mortgage's 100% digital flow responds to leads in seconds. Local brokers can't beat the tech — but AI Sales Pipeline closes the gap for the 60% of borrowers who want local expertise AND instant response.

Pre-qualification eats LO time

A loan officer can spend 30 minutes on a pre-qual call that doesn't result in an application. AI collects the same info in 5 minutes of back-and-forth, so LOs only meet already-pre-qualified buyers.

Purchase referrals from realtors are time-sensitive

Frequently asked questions

Can AI give an actual mortgage quote?

AI gives a preliminary estimate based on rough inputs (credit range self-reported, income, down payment). Final rate and approval requires pulling credit and running through the LOS — which still needs a licensed loan officer for compliance. The AI's job is to deliver a directionally-correct number in 5 minutes, then book the LO meeting for the binding quote.

How does AI compete with Rocket Mortgage's UX?

By matching the speed while keeping the local relationship. Rocket wins on speed and UX; it loses when borrowers want a human to explain a non-standard situation (self-employed income, recent credit event, investment property math). AI Sales Pipeline gives you Rocket's response speed, then hands to you for the relationship work — which is where local brokers actually beat Rocket.

Does AI Sales Pipeline integrate with Encompass/Calyx?

Yes. Leads flow in from your marketing channels, get qualified in the AI, then push to Encompass or Calyx as a 1003-ready record with pre-qualification notes. The loan officer picks up a lead that's already partially completed and ready for full application.

How does AI handle sensitive income conversations?

Income and DTI conversations happen via secure text/email, not voice — because borrowers are more candid in writing about self-employment quirks or recent job changes. The AI normalizes the conversation ('many of my best-approved borrowers are self-employed, so this is normal') to reduce the shame friction that makes borrowers under-report.

What about recycled leads from my past book?

Highest-ROI workflow for mortgage. Past borrowers move every 7-10 years; refi on 1/3 of 10-year time. Your 500-closing book has ~50-75 transactions in it each year you're not capturing because no one has time to call. AI runs annual outreach: home-value update, current-rate comparison, purchase-power-now estimate. Typical result: 5-12% of contacted past clients book a new deal within 12 months.

Does AI Sales Pipeline work for commercial or investment mortgages?

Partially. Residential-purchase and refi workflows are well-supported. Commercial and investment (DSCR, fix-and-flip, bridge) need more custom workflow — the qualification logic is different (DSCR uses rental income projections, not W2 income). We support commercial on the Elite tier with custom configuration.
