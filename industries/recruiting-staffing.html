Why recruiting & staffing agencys need AI-driven follow-up

Candidates disappear in 48 hours

Passive candidates respond to recruiters for 24-48 hours, then ghost. Speed-to-first-touch defines who wins the placement.

Frequently asked questions

Can AI screen candidates without bias?

AI is trained specifically to NOT screen on protected characteristics (age, race, gender, religion, national origin, disability, family status). Screening is job-related: salary, location, skills, availability, JD-specific must-haves. EEO compliance is built-in.

Does AI work for executive search or only high-volume recruiting?

Both. Executive search uses AI for administrative (scheduling, follow-up, status updates to clients), not candidate evaluation. High-volume recruiting uses AI for actual pre-screening. Both models supported.

Can AI handle candidate callbacks and follow-up?

Yes — this is where recruiters lose candidates. AI runs the 'checking in, still interested?' cadence over 30-60 days. Passive candidates who weren't ready at first contact often move on within that window; AI catches them at the right moment.

How does AI handle client communications?

Automated weekly status updates ('roles filled, roles in progress, candidates pending decision') go to hiring managers. Response required items route to the recruiter. AI writes in the recruiter's voice based on recorded past communications.
