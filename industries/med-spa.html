Frequently asked questions

Is AI HIPAA-compliant for med spa?

Yes — the Elite tier includes a BAA (Business Associate Agreement) and HIPAA-appropriate data handling. Medical history collection is encrypted, audit-logged, and stored only as long as required. Standard aesthetic consultations (Botox, filler, basic laser) operate under HIPAA-light — the BAA is needed when you're collecting extensive medical history.

Can AI handle pre-consultation medical intake?

Yes — the AI walks patients through medical history questions conversationally via text, which patients prefer to a paper form. Responses auto-populate the patient record in Zenoti/Boulevard. Sensitive questions (pregnancy, autoimmune conditions) are asked in a non-judgmental tone that increases disclosure.

How does AI share pricing without scaring patients off?

AI shares starting pricing transparently ('Botox starts at $12/unit, typical treatment is 20-40 units') and explains the consult is where personalized quoting happens. This approach converts 20-30% better than 'pricing available at consult only' because patients self-qualify before the appointment.

Does AI upsell or cross-sell appropriately?

Gently and contextually. After a Botox treatment, AI might send (14 days later): 'How's the Botox treating you? A lot of our Botox patients love filler for lip or cheek volume — would you like to schedule a consult to discuss?' The offer is educational, not pushy — which increases acceptance and doesn't damage the relationship.

Can AI sell memberships or packages?

Yes. For patients who've had 2+ treatments of the same type, AI can offer the relevant package or membership with package math ('3 laser sessions is $1,200 individually; our package is $900 for 3'). Typical result: 15-25% of eligible patients convert to membership within 30 days of the offer.

How does AI handle no-shows?

AI sends 24-hour and 2-hour reminders, confirms the appointment, and if patient cancels or no-shows, automatically offers a reschedule with the next 5 available slots. For chronic no-shows (2+ in a quarter), AI can require a card-on-file deposit for future bookings.
