Frequently asked questions

Is AI Sales Pipeline compliant with legal ethics rules?

Yes, when configured correctly. The AI does not provide legal advice — it collects facts and qualifies cases for attorney review. This fits within the Rules of Professional Conduct because paralegals have long done this same task. AI templates include required disclosures ('this is not legal advice, an attorney will review your case') and state-specific solicitation disclosures.

Can AI close cases or just intake them?

AI handles intake and scheduling; the actual signing happens with a human (attorney or licensed paralegal) per state ethics rules. For high-volume firms, AI can handle retainer e-signature logistics after the attorney has approved the case, but the case-acceptance decision is always human.

Does AI work for medical malpractice, products liability, and other complex PI?

Yes — with deeper qualification. Med mal requires screening for the standard-of-care threshold, statute nuances, and medical-record availability. Products liability needs product info, injury chain. AI handles the initial screening; complex cases always route to attorney for full review.

How does AI handle referral fees from non-qualifying cases?

AI identifies referable cases (out-of-state, outside practice area, volume-limit declines) and routes to your referral partner network. Intro email goes out same-day; tracking ensures referral fee is documented if the partner firm signs and closes. Typical firm captures $20K-$80K/year in referral revenue that was previously lost.

What about spam and fake leads?

AI screens for obvious spam/fraud signals: incident dates that don't match (e.g., claiming 2020 injury for recent outreach), implausible damages, fake names. For marginal cases, AI still collects full info; your intake team reviews and closes the loop. Not all spam is caught, but AI reduces the volume attorneys see by 40-60%.

How long does setup take for a PI firm?

10-14 days. Configuration includes: state-specific ethics rule templates, your practice area and geographic jurisdiction setup, case management tool integration, referral partner network, and qualification screening logic calibrated to your intake criteria.
