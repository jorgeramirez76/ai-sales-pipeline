Frequently asked questions

How does AI handle the insurance-vs-retail split?

Within the first 2-3 messages, AI asks 'are you going through insurance for this, or paying out of pocket?' The answer determines the workflow. Insurance: collect claim #, adjuster info, date of loss, schedule inspection with adjuster present when possible. Retail: collect roof details, offer transparent pricing ranges, schedule inspection for firm quote.

Can AI handle the adjuster coordination?

Partially. AI can schedule the inspection to match the adjuster's calendar (when claim is open), send the adjuster appointment confirmations, and document communications. The actual claim negotiation (supplement discussions, scope disputes) stays with your production manager or estimator.

What about canvasser lead follow-up?

This is where AI makes the biggest dent. Canvassers collect leads; AI runs the follow-up sequence. Typical result: 10-25% of canvasser leads that would have gone cold convert to booked inspections. For a canvasser doing 40 leads/day, that's 4-10 additional bookings per crew per day.

Does AI work for commercial roofing?

Partially. Light commercial (HOAs, small office) works well. Industrial/large commercial is relationship-driven, RFP-heavy, and AI's role is limited to administrative. Most commercial roofing contractors use AI for residential + light commercial only.

How does AI handle storm-season surge?

Unlimited concurrent capacity. When a hail event hits your market, lead volume can 10x in 72 hours. AI handles all of it — no lost leads, no waiting calls. Your inspection crews get booked solid; canvassers can be redirected to doors that haven't already been contacted.

Can AI handle Google LSA (Local Services Ads)?

Yes — and Google LSA performance depends heavily on response speed. AI Sales Pipeline responds to LSA leads in under 60 seconds, which keeps your LSA cost-per-lead low and your booking rate high. Without AI, slow LSA response = LSA suspends your ads for poor performance.
