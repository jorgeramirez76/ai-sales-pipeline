Frequently asked questions

Do you support industries not on this list?

Yes — if your business has a predictable lead-follow-up problem (more than 30 inquiries per week where speed-to-response determines outcome), AI Sales Pipeline can be configured for it. Common non-listed industries we've built for: financial advisors, law firms (personal injury), roofing, cleaning services, SaaS inside sales, wedding venues, fitness/gym sales. Tell us your industry and we'll scope the build.

How long does industry-specific configuration take?

7-14 days for common industries (real estate, auto, insurance, mortgage). 14-21 days for newer verticals where we haven't built before. The extra time is spent learning your specific workflow, compliance rules, and customer language.

Can AI Sales Pipeline handle multi-location businesses?

Yes — on the Elite tier. We set up per-location routing (lead goes to the right branch), shared reporting across locations, and unified compliance monitoring. Pricing scales per location but with volume discounts after the second site.
