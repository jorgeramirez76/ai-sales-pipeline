Frequently asked questions

Does AI work for big-box gyms vs boutique studios?

Both, with different workflows. Big-box gyms focus on volume tours and membership close; AI runs the funnel. Boutique studios (Orangetheory, F45, yoga studios) focus on intro-class conversion; AI handles intro booking and first-class reminders, then runs the 'how was it?' sequence to convert to membership.

How does AI handle the January New Year surge?

Unlimited concurrent capacity. Normal volume 20 inquiries/week; January peaks at 100-200/week. Your front desk can only handle maybe 40. AI handles all 200, books tours into schedules, runs the pre-tour nurture. Typical January gym using AI signs up 2-3x more members than prior years.

Can AI run personal training sales?

AI handles the intake and booking ('would you like a PT intro session?'), but the actual PT close is done by the trainer in-person after the intro session. The trainer's job is to coach and close; AI's job is to put people in front of the trainer.

Does AI handle class booking?

Yes — but class booking is usually handled by the gym's own app (Mindbody/Glofox has its own booking UI). AI handles the questions around class booking: 'what time is yoga tomorrow?' 'is there a class under 45 minutes?' — questions that would otherwise overwhelm front desk.

What about cancellation saves?

AI handles the first layer — 'I'd love to understand what made you want to cancel, is there anything we can adjust?' For real saves (billing issues, schedule conflicts), AI routes to the manager. For 'I'm moving' or 'injury', AI handles the cancellation cleanly. Typical result: 15-25% of cancellation attempts are saved or converted to a hold rather than full cancellation.

How much does this cost for a gym?

Single-location gyms: $495-$995/mo. Multi-location chains: custom-quoted. PT-heavy studios often see fastest ROI because PT sales have high margins.
