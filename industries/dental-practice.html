Frequently asked questions

Is AI HIPAA-compliant for dental?

Yes — Elite tier includes BAA and HIPAA-appropriate data handling. Medical history intake is encrypted. For general new-patient booking (no health data yet), HIPAA doesn't apply; once health data enters the conversation, Elite-tier handling kicks in.

Can AI verify insurance accurately?

For major insurance carriers with API access (Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, Guardian), AI can do real-time eligibility checks with high accuracy. For smaller or union plans, AI works from your participation list and flags anything uncertain to the front desk.

Does AI work for specialty practices (ortho, perio, endo)?

Yes — with specialty-specific workflows. Ortho has long treatment cycles and observation visits; perio needs periodontal maintenance recall. Endo is referral-heavy and has specific case intake needs. We configure per specialty.

How does AI handle dental anxiety and patient concerns?

AI uses calm, educational tone for anxious patients. For specific fears (sedation, cost, pain), AI provides reassurance + directs to the doctor's consultation. The AI doesn't 'sell through' anxiety — it acknowledges and books the consultation where the dentist can address it in person.

Can AI handle treatment plan follow-up without being pushy?

Yes — the key is the messaging tone. Instead of 'you still have $3,000 in unscheduled treatment,' the AI sends: 'Hi Sarah, hope you're doing well. I noticed your crown on #14 was planned but not scheduled. Would you like me to book the first appointment?' It's service-oriented, not sales-pushy. Typical result: 25-40% of unscheduled plans get booked via AI outreach.
