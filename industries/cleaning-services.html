Why cleaning services (residential & commercial)s need AI-driven follow-up

Residential inquiries are impulse

Moving out, pre-listing, deep clean before guests — residential cleaning is usually 'book this week' urgency. Fast response wins.

Commercial RFPs need systematic response

Office cleaning RFPs are competitive. First-responder advantage is real but usually less than residential.

Recurring client churn

Residential weekly/biweekly clients churn 15-25%/year for reasons like 'we moved' or 'we're handling it ourselves.' Proactive retention outreach catches many saves.

Quote requests from unqualified jobs

'How much to clean my 4,000 sq ft house with 6 dogs weekly?' is a quote the owner takes 10 min to produce. AI gives an instant estimate from your pricing tiers.

Frequently asked questions

Can AI quote accurately without seeing the home?

Residential: yes for standard homes (AI asks square footage, bedrooms, bathrooms, frequency). Unusual situations (hoarding cleanup, move-out deep clean with significant damage) get 'custom quote requires 5-minute site visit or video call.' Commercial: always custom quote.

Does AI handle cancellations?

Yes. When a client cancels a recurring plan, AI attempts a save sequence: 'would pausing for 30 days work better than canceling?' or 'would a different frequency work for your budget?' Typical result: 20-30% of cancellation attempts are saved.

Can AI handle multi-location franchises?

Yes — Elite tier supports multi-location with per-location routing (new customer goes to nearest franchisee), shared brand standards, unified reporting.

What about bond cleaning / move-out deep clean?

Handled via flat-rate pricing (if you publish tiered rates) or custom quote triggering for unusual scope. AI asks the right questions to triage: standard-move-out vs deep-clean vs damage-remediation.
