AI SMS

What It Does: AI SMS transforms lead outreach by delivering natural, conversational text messages that feel like they're coming from a friend, not a bot. The system reads incoming lead responses in real-time and adapts its tone, urgency, and messaging strategy based on what the lead is saying. If someone's excited, the AI matches that energy. If they're hesitant, it smoothly handles objections with social proof and value propositions that actually work.

How It Works Technically: When a new lead arrives (from Zillow, Facebook, your website, or any source), AI SMS instantly sends a personalized first message. As the lead responds, the AI analyzes their sentiment, urgency signals, and pain points using natural language processing. It then selects the best response from thousands of proven templates—but personalizes each one with the lead's name, property details, and context from your previous conversations. No two messages are identical because every lead is different.

Why It Benefits You: You never miss a lead again because AI responds 24/7, even while you're sleeping. The average response time is under 45 seconds, which dramatically increases your chances of grabbing their attention before a competitor does. Because messages feel genuinely human, leads stay engaged longer and are more likely to book a call or showing with you.

How It Helps You

  • Never miss a lead, no matter what time they inquire
  • Respond faster than competitors—45 seconds vs hours
  • Messages feel human, so leads don't feel like they're texting a robot
  • AI handles objections automatically without your involvement
  • Qualify leads while you sleep, then focus only on hot prospects

Real World Example

It's 2:17 AM on a Tuesday. Someone in your farm area clicks "Request Agent" on Zillow for a $425K starter home. Your phone buzzes. It's not you—it's AI Sales Pipeline. Within 45 seconds, a natural text lands in their pocket: "Hi Sarah! 👋 Thanks for checking out the Maple St property! I specialize in first-time buys in this area and have closed 12 in the last 6 months. Quick question—are you pre-approved yet?" Sarah wakes up at 7 AM and sees your message. She responds: "Not yet." AI immediately texts back with mortgage pre-approval resources and books a time for a call. By 10 AM, you're on a call with a qualified buyer while your competitor is still asleep. Deal closed 45 days later.

AI Email

What It Does: AI Email creates personalized, behavior-triggered email sequences that automatically adapt based on what your leads are actually doing. Instead of sending the same generic message to everyone, this system watches for opens, clicks, and engagement patterns, then serves the perfect follow-up at the perfect moment. Cold, warm, or hot—each lead gets a customized sequence designed to move them toward booking a showing or listing appointment.

How It Works Technically: Every email is built with intelligent HTML that tracks opens and clicks without being creepy about it. When a lead receives your first email, AI monitors whether they opened it, what links they clicked, and how quickly they engaged. If they opened it but didn't click anything, they get a different follow-up than someone who clicked the property link twice. The system runs A/B tests automatically, learning which subject lines, body copy, and calls-to-action work best with each segment. Plain text emails go to leads who prefer conversational tone; HTML emails with property photos go to visual learners.

Why It Benefits You: Email has the highest ROI of any marketing channel when done right—and AI Email does it right. By sending the right message at the right time based on actual behavior, you see 3x higher open rates than industry average and 2x higher click-through rates. More importantly, leads feel like you're actually listening to them, not just blasting the same template to thousands of people.

How It Helps You

  • 3x higher open rates than industry standard email
  • Automatic follow-ups based on lead behavior—no manual sequencing
  • Inbox-safe delivery with AI-optimized sending times
  • Re-engage cold leads with perfectly timed "checking in" campaigns
  • Detailed tracking shows exactly which content converts leads to calls

Real World Example

You generated a hot lead from your website 3 weeks ago. They were interested in a 4BR colonial but the market was competitive and they went quiet. Normally you'd have to manually decide: should I follow up? What do I say? Instead, AI Email knows. It automatically sends them a "Hey, we found something you'll love" email with a new listing that matches their saved search perfectly. If they open it, AI follows up within 2 hours with a video walkthrough link. If they clicked the video link, AI triggers a personal text from you. If they ignore it, AI automatically adds them to a "warm nurture" sequence that stays top-of-mind without being pushy. Two months later, they respond to one of those emails. You call, schedule a showing, and close on a different (but equally beautiful) colonial. That close happened because AI never let them leave your pipeline.

AI Voice

What It Does: AI Voice is your tireless outbound calling machine. It places calls 24/7, speaks with natural conversational tone, handles objections in real-time, and qualifies leads without your involvement. When a lead answers, AI has a natural conversation that sounds like a seasoned agent—not a robo-call. It asks qualifying questions, listens to answers, and knows when to pivot the conversation toward booking an appointment or getting more information. If it reaches a voicemail, it leaves a natural message (no "this is a call from—" robotic nonsense) and logs everything for your review.

How It Works Technically: AI Voice uses advanced conversational AI that understands context, intent, and emotion. When you load a batch of phone numbers into the system, AI creates a calling queue and works through them intelligently. If a lead is aggressive, AI's tone shifts to be more professional and respectful. If they're curious, AI matches that energy and explores their needs deeper. The system integrates directly with your calendar, so when a lead says "I'm available next Tuesday at 2 PM," AI checks your real availability, confirms the appointment, and sends a calendar invite—all in the same conversation. Calls are recorded and transcribed, so you have a full record of what was discussed before you call them back.

Why It Benefits You: Phone calls still convert better than any other channel, but most agents avoid cold calling because it's time-consuming and emotionally draining. AI Voice does all the heavy lifting. You get 391% higher contact rates than email alone because AI is relentless—it tries optimal times, leaves smart voicemails, and follows up at the best moments. Meanwhile, you're focusing on hot leads and actual closings.

How It Helps You

  • 391% higher contact rate than email-only outreach
  • Call 100+ people per day while you focus on hot prospects
  • AI handles objections and qualifies leads automatically
  • Integrated calendar booking—appointments happen mid-conversation
  • Full call transcripts and recordings for your review and coaching

Real World Example

Normally, you'd have to choose: show homes or make calls. You choose both. Before you leave home, you upload the 50 numbers to AI Voice with your opening script: "Hi [name], I just noticed your property came off market—I specialize in expired listings and have sold 8 just like yours in the last 90 days." AI gets to work. While you're showing home #3, AI has already called all 50 numbers, reached 14 people, scheduled 3 callbacks with interested sellers, and left smart voicemails for the rest. By Sunday morning, 3 sellers are ready to talk to you about relisting. Two weeks later, you have a signed listing agreement worth $18K commission. Meanwhile, AI was on those calls for a grand total of $28. That's the power of AI Voice.

Then, for each lead, it generates a unique, personalized video of YOU saying things specific to THEM. The video shows you on camera saying their name, referencing the property they looked at, acknowledging something they said in a previous conversation, and inviting them to book a call. Each video is different—it's not a template, it's a fresh video generated uniquely for that person. Leads see a link in an SMS or email, click it, and watch what feels like a genuine personal video message from you.

How It Works Technically: When you sign up, you record 5-10 minutes of natural footage of yourself talking, smiling, nodding, showing different expressions. AI analyzes your facial structure, voice patterns, accent, and mannerisms. Then, for every lead, you write a short 2-3 sentence personalization ("Hey Michael, saw you looked at the 3BR colonial on Elm Street 3 times last week—I think it's perfect for you. AI generates a unique 30-60 second video with your digital likeness saying that exact script, with natural gestures and expressions that match your training footage. The video is hosted via a link sent in SMS or email. When leads click it, they see what looks like a real video of you personally reaching out.

Why It Benefits You: Video gets 8x higher engagement than text messages. But sending a personal video to every lead would take you 80 hours per week. AI Clone Video does it in minutes. Leads see it as incredibly personal and high-touch—most agents don't do this. You stand out immediately. The psychological impact of seeing a face (even if it's AI-generated) is massive. Leads feel like you specifically reached out to them, not a bot.

How It Helps You

  • Feels like a personal video from you—not a template
  • Leads remember your face and personality better than words
  • Dramatically increases call-back rates from cold and warm leads
  • Takes minutes to create—not hours of video recording and editing

Lead Scoring (Cold/Warm/Hot)

What It Does: Lead Scoring is AI's behind-the-scenes intelligence system that rates every lead on a scale of 0-100 (Cold, Warm, or Hot) based on their actual behavior. Instead of guessing which leads are ready to move and which are just browsing, AI analyzes response speed, word sentiment, pages visited, email open patterns, link clicks, calendar responsiveness, and buying urgency signals. A lead who opens an email within 2 minutes and clicks the property link 3 times is clearly Hot. A lead who read one email but hasn't engaged since? Cold. The system continuously updates scores as behavior changes, so you always know exactly who to prioritize.

How It Works Technically: When a lead enters your pipeline, AI starts collecting data immediately. Response time? Recorded. Sentiment of their messages (excited, hesitant, defensive)? Analyzed. Pages visited on your website? Tracked. Email opens and clicks? Logged. If they opened an email at 10 PM on a weeknight (instead of during work hours), that's a signal of higher interest. If they asked about mortgage pre-approval, that's a Hot signal. If they viewed the same property 5 times in a week? Very Hot. AI assigns a numeric score (0-100) and a label (Cold/Warm/Hot). It also flags specific behavioral triggers—like "clicked price-reduced listing" or "responded with a question." These triggers tell you exactly why the AI scored them that way, so you can prioritize your follow-up strategy.

Why It Benefits You: Most agents spend time equally on all leads, which is a massive waste. With Lead Scoring, you know exactly who to call first. You spend 70% of your time on Hot leads (who are ready to move), 20% on Warm leads (who need nurturing), and 10% on Cold leads (who need time). This focus dramatically increases your close rate and saves you from wasting hours on people who aren't ready.

How It Helps You

  • Know instantly which leads are ready to buy/sell vs just browsing
  • Eliminate guesswork—decisions based on actual behavior data
  • Spend more time on Hot leads, less time on tire-kickers
  • Discover high-intent leads before your competitors even know they exist
  • Auto-route leads to workflows—Hot leads get special treatment

Real World Example

You have 87 leads in your pipeline. Normally, you'd have to manually review each one and decide who to call. You have maybe 2 hours, so 1-2 minutes per lead max. With Lead Scoring, you open your dashboard and immediately see: 4 Hot leads (responses within 2 minutes, opened 3+ emails, viewed properties 5+ times), 23 Warm leads (opened emails, slower to respond, some link clicks), and 60 Cold leads (single opens, no clicks, minimal engagement). You spend 90 minutes calling the 4 Hot leads. All 4 answer. 3 of them are genuinely ready to move. Two weeks later, you're in showings with all 3. One closes on a buyer's side deal worth $12K commission. The system identified those 4 golden leads from your pool of 87. Without Lead Scoring, you'd have been randomly dialing and hoping. Instead, you hit the jackpot with 100% focus.

Facebook Retargeting

What It Does: Facebook Retargeting ensures that leads who engage with your content but don't convert keep seeing you—at scale and for a fraction of the cost of cold Facebook ads. When someone visits your website, a tracking pixel fires. AI builds a custom audience of everyone who engaged (clicked property links, watched video, spent 30+ seconds on a page). Then, AI automatically creates and serves targeted ads only to that warm audience. Instead of spending money advertising to the entire platform (most of whom will never buy/sell), you spend only on people who've already shown interest in you. The cost-per-lead drops dramatically.

How It Works Technically: We place a small pixel on your website (standard Facebook Pixel). When a lead visits, that pixel records them. AI groups visitors by behavior: those who looked at listings (warm audience), those who opened your seller guide (hot audience), those who watched a video (very hot audience). Behind the scenes, AI continuously creates custom audiences in your Facebook Ad Manager. Then it automatically launches ads targeted ONLY to each segment. A lead who opened 3 seller videos gets served an ad with testimonials from successful sellers. A lead who browsed listings but didn't click gets served an ad highlighting a new listing in their area. Everything is automatic and rules-based—you just set your monthly budget and let AI manage the rest.

Why It Benefits You: The CPL (cost-per-lead) on cold Facebook ads is usually $15-30. With retargeting, it drops to $1.50-3 because you're advertising only to warm audiences. Even better, when warm leads see your ad on Facebook, they're reminded of you. They click through, which warms them up further. If they don't convert after the ad, they enter AI Email sequences. It's a coordinated attack that compounds your advantage.

How It Helps You

  • 10x lower CPL than cold Facebook ads—only spend on warm leads
  • Automatic audience building—pixel tracks behavior, AI builds segments
  • Stay top-of-mind with leads who went cold after initial interest
  • Perfect complement to AI Email and SMS—multi-touch campaign
  • Detailed ROI tracking—know exactly how much each ad costs you to close

Real World Example

Marcus opened 3 of your seller guide emails last month, watched a 4-minute video on how to price your home (signal of a serious seller), but then went dark. You assume he's lost. Wrong. The next time Marcus scrolls Facebook, he sees an ad from you featuring a testimonial from another seller who increased their list price by $22K after taking your advice. Something clicks. He clicks the ad. Returns to your website. Looks at 3 comparable sales you pulled for his neighborhood. This time, he fills out a form asking for a free CMA (comparative market analysis). You jump on a call, book an appointment, and list his home 3 days later at the right price. It sells in 5 days at $18K over asking. Commission: $19,800. That entire sequence was triggered by Facebook retargeting a lead who had gone cold. The ad cost you $47 to generate that lead. Best $47 you ever spent.

Seller Workflows

What It Does: Seller Workflows are pre-built, customizable sequences designed for different seller types. A FSBO (For Sale By Owner) needs a different message than someone with an expired listing. A divorcing couple needs different messaging than empty-nesters downsizing. Instead of using one generic "hey, list with me" sequence for everyone, you have laser-focused workflows that address each seller's specific situation, pain point, and motivation. Each workflow includes battle-tested scripts, objection handlers, and a clear path to a listing appointment.

How It Works Technically: You choose which sellers to target and select their workflow: FSBO, Expired Listing, Downsizer, Commuter, Divorce/Estate/Relocation. AI populates the sequence with personalized messaging. For FSBO sellers, AI uses the NEPQ approach (educating them on why they actually need an agent). For expired listings, AI leads with market analysis and a new pricing strategy. For downsizers, AI focuses on lifestyle and simplicity. Each workflow is a 7-12 touch sequence combining SMS, email, video, and calls. You can customize the sequence, swap out messaging, or keep it as-is. When a lead goes through the workflow, they get the right message at the right time, leading naturally toward a listing consultation call.

Why It Benefits You: Pre-built workflows mean you don't have to reinvent the wheel. Each one is designed based on what actually works with that seller type. You get consistent, high-quality outreach. And because the messaging is targeted to their specific situation, sellers feel understood and are more likely to take your call.

How It Helps You

  • Pre-built sequences proven to work with different seller types
  • Right message to right seller—NEPQ for FSBO, CMA for expired, etc.
  • Fully customizable—add your scripts, change timing, adjust messaging
  • Automates the entire sequence—you just manage the appointments
  • Sellers feel personally understood, not generically pitched

Buyer Workflows

What It Does: Buyer Workflows automatically qualify, educate, and nurture incoming buyer leads without requiring your personal involvement. When a buyer fills out a form or calls your number, AI immediately assesses their budget, timeline, motivation, and pre-approval status through a series of conversational questions. It matches them to neighborhoods that fit their criteria, sends pre-approval resources if they're unqualified, and books showings directly on your calendar when they're ready. By the time they talk to you, they're already pre-qualified and you're not wasting time on someone who isn't serious.

How It Works Technically: A buyer inquiry arrives (website form, phone call, or text). AI immediately engages with a natural conversation: "What price range are you looking at? Timeline? Do you have a pre-approval letter yet?" Based on their answers, AI scores their qualification level. If they're pre-approved with a specific timeline, they're Hot and get immediately routed to showing scheduling. If they're not pre-approved, AI sends them to a lender partner's pre-approval page and follows up in 24 hours. If their timeline is "someday," AI adds them to a nurture sequence and checks back monthly. Everything is rules-based but sounds conversational. You get a dashboard notification only when a genuinely qualified buyer is ready for showings.

Why It Benefits You: Most agents spend 60% of their time on unqualified buyers who have no pre-approval, no real timeline, and no serious intent. Buyer Workflows filter for you. You only get notified when someone is actually ready to move. This shifts your time from tire-kickers to closings. You'll close more deals with the same or less effort.

How It Helps You

  • Auto-qualify buyers—know budget, timeline, and pre-approval status immediately
  • Only get notified when someone is genuinely ready to buy
  • Automatic showing scheduling—no phone tag, no back-and-forth
  • Lead nurturing for future buyers (someday buyers stay warm)
  • Increase pre-approval rates—AI sends resources and follows up

AI immediately sends them a link to a pre-approval calculator and a trusted lender partner. 24 hours later, AI checks back: "Any updates on pre-approval?" They filled out the pre-approval form. AI runs their info through its scoring system: genuine buyer, legitimate finances, 60-90 day timeline = HOT. AI texts: "Great! AI books it directly on your calendar with a reminder 2 hours before. You show them 3 homes, one is perfect, they love it, you write an offer that night. Deal closes 30 days later.
